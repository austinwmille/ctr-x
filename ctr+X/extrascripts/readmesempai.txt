This folder will contain (and does contain) separate scripts.

These are technically unrelated to the goal of this project, i.e. clipme.py

They just do very simple things to a video using ffmpeg, imagemagick, or whatever.

These, in general, don't require access to large AI models, and are exponentially quicker.

These include resizing, cropping, adding text or decorations, crawling the web for content, adding subtitles (when we don't need a LLM to transcribe), etc.
